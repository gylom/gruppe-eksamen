ChopChopShop UX exploration

Six warm, mobile-first directions for the household food loop

Each direction keeps the same product core: a shared weekly meal plan becomes a trusted shopping list, then completed trips become cookbook history.

Design foundation Warm-neutral base, muted terracotta accent, sage success, amber caution, light and dark token support. Interaction anchor Bottom navigation, bottom sheets, swipe rows, transparent generated suggestions, reversible shopping actions.

Warm Routine

The default candidate: balanced, calm, and very close to the target app. It gives the weekly plan enough structure without making it feel like a calendar product.

Best all-rounder Plan first Calm hierarchy
  • Strong fit for `/app/planned` and generated suggestions.
  • Uses terracotta only for action, selection, and progress.
  • Good base direction for MVP implementation.

Shared Market

A shopping-first interpretation inspired by AnyList. It emphasizes grouped rows, attribution, and one-handed completion.

Shopping clarity Grouped rows Sage accent
  • Excellent for the store-use moment.
  • Aisle grouping can stay visual-only for MVP if backend grouping is not ready.
  • Risks making the product feel list-first instead of planning-first.
